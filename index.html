BINGE!

Watch Netflix without getting interrupted.

Marathoning all seven seasons of that show in one night? Tired of Netflix pretending like you're not actually watching? (c'mon now). Annoyed by the interruptions three minutes into an episode?? Add this extension and stop being bothered.

Chrome Extension

No configuration options, just install and keep the shows playing.

Bookmarklet
